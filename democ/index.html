National Popular Vote Interstate Compact

by: Vlad Kolotnikov

So, what is NPVIC Briefly?

The National Popular Vote Interstate Compact, commonly abrieviated as NPVIC is an agreement between states to vote for the candidate with the highest percentage vote nationwide during the presidential election. There are many justifications and condemnations, pros and cons, support and opposition to this compact. While it is not in effect yet, seeing as the compact does not yet have majority votes required for it to be successful, many states have NPVIC legislature either active or pending.
